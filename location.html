About the Location

The Japanese Garden is located within the Tashkent Botanical Garden, one of the largest and most beautiful green spaces in Uzbekistan. Situated in the Yunusabad district of Tashkent, it is easily accessible from the city center and is well-connected by public transportation.

Address: Japanese Garden, Tashkent Botanical Garden, Kibray District, Tashkent, Uzbekistan.

Landmarks: The garden is near several popular landmarks, including the Tashkent TV Tower and the UzExpoCenter. It’s a short drive from Amir Temur Square and is ideal for those looking to combine cultural and natural attractions.

Getting There: Visitors can reach the Japanese Garden by:

  • By Car: Ample parking is available near the Tashkent Botanical Garden.
  • By Metro: The nearest metro station is "Bodomzor," located approximately 1.5 kilometers from the garden.
  • By Bus: Multiple bus routes stop near the garden. Look for buses heading towards the Botanical Garden or Yunusabad district.
